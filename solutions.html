Frequently asked questions

How does Pi Network payment integration work?

Sentry provides a simple API that you can integrate into your website or app. When a customer chooses to pay with Pi, our system generates a payment request that connects to their Pi wallet. Once the payment is confirmed on the Pi blockchain, we notify your system and update the order status.

How are reviews verified on the blockchain?

When a customer submits a review, we verify their purchase history and identity (with their permission). The review is then cryptographically signed and stored on a blockchain with a timestamp and verification status. This creates an immutable record that cannot be altered, giving confidence to future customers about the authenticity of the review.

What types of analytics does Sentry provide?

Sentry's analytics platform provides insights on payment trends, conversion rates, customer behavior, review sentiment, and more. You can access real-time dashboards, custom reports, and data visualizations to help you make informed business decisions.

Is Sentry secure and compliant with regulations?

Yes, security is our top priority. Sentry uses enterprise-grade encryption, follows industry best practices for data protection, and is designed to comply with relevant financial and data protection regulations. We continuously update our security measures to address emerging threats.

How quickly can I get started with Sentry?

Most businesses can be up and running with Sentry in less than a day. Our simple API, comprehensive documentation, and step-by-step guides make integration straightforward. Our support team is also available to help you with any questions during the setup process.
