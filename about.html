About Us

Our Vision

Building the foundational financial infrastructure for the Pi Network's burgeoning commerce ecosystem. We aim to empower businesses and consumers alike by providing secure, seamless, and innovative tools that foster trust and growth within the Pi economy.

Meet Our Team

Sentry is driven by a dedicated team of developers, designers, blockchain experts, and passionate Pi Network enthusiasts. We are committed to building high-quality, user-centric solutions for the Pi community.

Biographies and photos of our core team members are coming soon! We're excited for you to meet the people behind Sentry.

Our Alignment with Pi Network

Sentry is developed with the core principles of the Pi Network in mind. We believe in Pi's mission to build a cryptocurrency and smart contracts platform secured and operated by everyday people. Our services are designed to enhance the utility of Pi by enabling real-world commerce and creating a vibrant ecosystem for Pi users and businesses.

We are committed to:

  • Accessibility: Making our tools easy to use for everyone, regardless of technical expertise.
  • Security: Prioritizing the safety and security of all transactions and data within our platform.
  • Innovation: Continuously developing new features and services that add value to the Pi Network.
  • Community Focus: Listening to the needs of the Pi community and building solutions that address them.

Sentry operates as an independent entity building upon the Pi Network blockchain and is not directly affiliated with the Pi Core Team, but we share a common vision for the future of Pi.
