Verified Reviews

Authenticity Guaranteed by the Blockchain

Sentry's Verified Reviews system leverages the power of the Pi blockchain to ensure that every review comes from a genuine customer. When a purchase is made using Sentry's payment processing, a unique transaction ID is generated on the Pi blockchain. Customers can then leave a review that is cryptographically linked to this transaction ID.

This process makes it virtually impossible for fake reviews to be posted, as only those who have completed a verified transaction can contribute. Say goodbye to anonymous, unverified feedback and hello to a new era of trust and transparency.

The Journey of a Verified Review

Step 1: Customer Makes a Purchase

A customer buys a product or service from your business using Sentry's Pi payment processing.

Step 2: Transaction ID Generated

A unique, immutable transaction ID is recorded on the Pi blockchain for that specific purchase.

Step 3: Customer Leaves a Review

After receiving their product/service, the customer is invited to leave a review through Sentry, linking it to their transaction ID.

Step 4: Review Verified & Displayed

Sentry verifies the review against the blockchain record. Once confirmed, the verified review is displayed on your website via our widget or API.
