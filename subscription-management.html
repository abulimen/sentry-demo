Subscription Management

Effortless Recurring Revenue with Pi

Sentry's Subscription Management system empowers businesses to offer services and content on a recurring basis, all powered by Pi cryptocurrency. Our platform automates the complexities of subscription billing, so you can focus on delivering value to your customers.

  • Recurring Billing: Set up daily, weekly, monthly, or annual billing cycles for your subscription plans. Payments are automatically attempted from the customer's linked Pi wallet.
  • Automated Invoice Generation: Professional, itemized invoices are automatically generated and sent to your customers for each billing period.
  • Customer Management Portal: Provide your customers with a secure portal where they can view their active subscriptions, update payment information (if applicable), and manage their subscription status (e.g., upgrade, downgrade, cancel).
  • Dunning Management: Our system automatically handles failed payments. Customizable retry logic and notifications help recover revenue and reduce churn. (Advanced dunning features coming soon).

Ideal for a Variety of Business Models

Whether you're a developer, creator, or service provider, Sentry's Subscription Management can adapt to your needs:

  • SaaS Companies: Offer your software applications on a subscription basis, paid in Pi.
  • Content Creators: Provide exclusive content (articles, videos, courses) to paying subscribers in the Pi community.
  • Membership-Based Businesses: Manage memberships for clubs, communities, or premium service tiers.
  • Service Providers: Offer ongoing services like website maintenance, regular consultations, or support packages with recurring Pi payments.
  • Gaming Platforms: Implement subscriptions for game access, in-game benefits, or season passes.

A Smooth Journey for Your Subscribers

We prioritize a clear and user-friendly experience for your customers when they subscribe to your offerings:

Step 1: Customer Selects a Plan

The customer chooses their desired subscription plan on your website or platform.

Step 2: Securely Links Pi Wallet

During the initial signup, the customer securely authorizes Sentry to initiate recurring payments from their Pi wallet for the agreed-upon subscription terms. This involves a clear consent process.

Step 3: Enjoys Your Service

Once subscribed, the customer gains access to your product or service.

Step 4: Manages Subscription via Portal

Customers can access their dedicated Sentry portal to view their subscription details, track payment history, and manage their plan (e.g., upgrade, cancel, subject to your terms).

Flexible Pricing for Your Offerings

Sentry supports various subscription pricing models to cater to different business needs:

  • Fixed Recurring: The most common model. Charge a flat fee at regular intervals (e.g., 10 Pi per month).
  • Tiered Pricing: Offer multiple subscription plans with different features and price points (e.g., Basic, Premium, Pro tiers).
  • Usage-Based Billing (Future): Charge customers based on their consumption of your service (e.g., per API call, per gigabyte of storage). This feature is planned for future release.
  • Freemium with Paid Upgrades: Offer a basic free plan with the option to upgrade to paid subscription tiers for more features.
