Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any setup fees or hidden charges?

No, Sentry believes in transparency. There are no setup fees for any of our plans. The pricing is as listed: a monthly fee for the Voyager plan (paid in Pi) and transaction fees. The Pioneer plan is free to start, with only transaction fees applicable.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, absolutely. You can easily change your Sentry plan (e.g., from Pioneer to Voyager or vice-versa) directly from your account dashboard as your business needs evolve. Prorated charges or credits will be applied where applicable.

How are the monthly subscription fees and transaction fees paid?

All Sentry subscription fees (for the Voyager plan) and transaction fees are settled in Pi. We aim to make the entire process seamless within the Pi Network ecosystem.

Do you offer discounts for high-volume transactions or non-profits?

For businesses with very high transaction volumes, our Enterprise plan offers custom volume-based rates. We are also open to discussing special considerations for registered non-profit organizations contributing to the Pi ecosystem. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific case.

Need a Custom Solution or Have More Questions?

If your business has unique requirements not covered by our standard plans, or if you have further questions about our pricing, our team is ready to help you find the perfect fit.
