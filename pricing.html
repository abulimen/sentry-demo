Frequently Asked Questions

How do transaction fees work?

Transaction fees are charged as a percentage of the total transaction amount plus a fixed fee. For example, with the Starter plan, a π100 transaction would incur a fee of π3.20 (2.9% of π100 + π0.30). Fees are only charged on successful transactions.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the new rate will take effect at the beginning of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my monthly transaction limit?

If you exceed your monthly transaction limit, you'll be charged a small overage fee per additional transaction. We'll send you notifications when you reach 80% and 100% of your limit. You can also upgrade to a higher plan at any time to avoid overage fees.

What's the difference between basic and advanced review verification?

Basic review verification confirms that the reviewer is a real customer who made a purchase. Advanced verification provides additional security by cryptographically signing reviews and storing them on the blockchain with a verification badge that can be displayed on your website. It also includes review analytics and fraud detection.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, contact our support team for a full refund. After 30 days, we do not offer prorated refunds for canceled subscriptions.
