Sentry Help Center

How can we help you today? Find answers quickly below or search our knowledge base.

Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Sentry and how does it relate to Pi Network?

Sentry is a third-party platform designed to empower businesses and developers by providing tools to easily integrate Pi Network payments into their websites, applications, and services. We also offer a unique blockchain-verified review system to enhance customer trust using the Pi blockchain's transparency.

Who can benefit from using Sentry's services?

Sentry is built for a wide range of users within the Pi Network ecosystem. This includes e-commerce store owners wanting to accept Pi, app developers looking to integrate Pi transactions, service providers invoicing in Pi, and any business aiming to build stronger customer trust through transparent, verified reviews.

Is Sentry an official part of the Pi Network?

Sentry is an independent platform developed to support and enhance the utility of the Pi Network. While we are dedicated to fostering the Pi ecosystem and operate in alignment with its principles, Sentry is not directly affiliated with the Pi Network Core Team.

What makes Sentry's blockchain-verified reviews different?

Our review system links customer feedback to actual transactions on the Pi blockchain. This creates an immutable, verifiable record that ensures reviews are genuine and resistant to manipulation, providing a higher level of trust compared to traditional review platforms.

Account Management

How do I create a Sentry account and what are the requirements?

You can create a Sentry account by visiting our Sign Up page. The process is straightforward, requiring a valid email address and a secure password. For businesses, some additional verification might be needed to access live payment processing features.

I've forgotten my Sentry account password. How can I reset it?

If you've forgotten your password, please go to the Log In page and click on the "Forgot Password?" link. You'll receive an email with instructions on how to securely reset your password.

Can I change my Sentry subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your Sentry subscription plan at any time directly from your account dashboard. Changes will typically take effect from the next billing cycle. Details on our plans can be found on the Pricing page.

Payments

What are the transaction fees for using Sentry's Pi payment gateway?

Our transaction fees are competitive and depend on the Sentry plan you choose. The Pioneer plan offers a simple percentage-based fee plus a small fixed Pi amount, while our Voyager plan has lower rates. For detailed fee structures, please visit our Pricing page.

How quickly are Pi payments confirmed through Sentry?

Sentry leverages the Pi blockchain for payment processing. Transactions are typically confirmed within seconds, providing a fast and efficient payment experience. However, confirmation times can occasionally vary based on overall Pi Network activity.

Does Sentry support Pi payment refunds?

Yes, Sentry provides merchants with tools within their dashboard to manage Pi transactions, including the ability to process refunds to customers when necessary. Detailed instructions can be found in our Managing Transactions guide.

Blockchain-Verified Reviews

How does Sentry ensure the authenticity of blockchain-verified reviews?

Sentry's system links review submissions to verified Pi transactions. A cryptographic hash of the review content and transaction data is then recorded on the Pi blockchain, creating a tamper-proof, transparent, and auditable record of authenticity.

Can I integrate and display Sentry's verified reviews on my own website or app?

Absolutely. Sentry offers easy-to-use widgets and API access, allowing you to seamlessly embed and display your blockchain-verified customer reviews on your product pages, testimonial sections, or any other digital platform, enhancing trust and social proof.

How does Sentry prevent manipulation or fake reviews?

By tying reviews to verified transactions and anchoring them to the Pi blockchain, Sentry significantly mitigates the risk of fake or manipulated reviews. The immutable nature of the blockchain record ensures that once a review is verified, it cannot be altered or deceptively represented.

Developer Support

Where can I find detailed API documentation for Sentry?

Our comprehensive API documentation, including endpoint details, request/response examples, and authentication guides, is available on our Developer Documentation page.

Does Sentry offer SDKs for easier integration with different programming languages?

Yes, Sentry is committed to providing a great developer experience. We offer official SDKs for popular languages (like JavaScript and Python) and are continuously working on expanding our SDK offerings. Check the Developer Tools section for the latest.

Is a sandbox or test environment available for Sentry API integrations?

Yes, Sentry provides a fully-featured sandbox environment that mirrors our live production system. This allows developers to thoroughly test their integrations with Pi payments and reviews using test API keys without interacting with real Pi currency. Details are in our API documentation.
