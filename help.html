Sentry Help Center

How can we help you today? Find answers quickly below or search our knowledge base.

Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Sentry?

Sentry is a comprehensive platform that enables businesses to seamlessly integrate Pi Network payments into their websites and applications. We also offer a unique blockchain-verified review system to enhance customer trust and provide valuable analytics.

Who is Sentry for?

Sentry is designed for any business, developer, or individual looking to engage with the Pi Network ecosystem. This includes e-commerce stores, service providers, app developers, and content creators who want to accept Pi payments or leverage transparent review mechanisms.

Is Sentry affiliated with the Pi Network Core Team?

Sentry is an independent platform built to support and enhance the Pi Network ecosystem. While we are dedicated to Pi, we are not directly affiliated with the Pi Network Core Team. We adhere to Pi Network's guidelines and aim to provide valuable services to the community.

Account Management

How do I create a Sentry account?

You can create a Sentry account by visiting our Sign Up page. The process is quick and requires only basic information to get started. Our Pioneer plan is free!

I forgot my password. What do I do?

If you've forgotten your password, you can click the "Forgot Password?" link on the Log In page. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset it.

How can I change my Sentry plan?

You can upgrade or change your Sentry plan at any time from your account dashboard. Navigate to the 'Billing' or 'Subscription' section once logged in. For more details on plans, see our Pricing page.

Payments

What are the transaction fees for using Sentry?

Transaction fees vary depending on your chosen Sentry plan. Our Pioneer plan has a fee of 2.0% + 0.05 Pi per transaction, while the Voyager plan offers a lower rate. Please visit our Pricing page for detailed information.

How long does it take for Pi payments to be confirmed?

Pi payments processed through Sentry are typically confirmed within seconds, leveraging the speed of the Pi blockchain. Confirmation times can vary slightly based on network conditions.

Can I issue refunds for Pi payments through Sentry?

Yes, Sentry provides tools within the merchant dashboard to manage transactions, including processing refunds for Pi payments where applicable. Please refer to our Guides section for more details on refund procedures.

Blockchain-Verified Reviews

How does the blockchain review verification work?

When a customer submits a review through Sentry after a verified transaction, key data points of that review are hashed and recorded on the Pi blockchain. This creates an immutable and transparent record, ensuring the review's authenticity and preventing tampering.

Can I display verified reviews on my own website?

Absolutely! Sentry provides widgets and API access to easily display your blockchain-verified reviews on your website, product pages, or any other digital property to build customer trust.

What prevents fake reviews with Sentry?

Our system primarily links reviews to verified transactions, significantly reducing the possibility of fake reviews. The blockchain verification adds another layer of authenticity, making it difficult to manipulate feedback.

Developer Support

Where can I find API documentation?

Our comprehensive API documentation is available on our Documentation page. It includes details on all endpoints, request/response formats, and authentication.

Do you offer SDKs for easier integration?

Yes, we are developing SDKs for various programming languages to simplify the integration process. Currently, we have information on our Developer Overview page, with links to available resources.

Is there a sandbox environment for testing?

Yes, Sentry provides a sandbox environment for developers to test their integrations without affecting live data or transactions. Details can be found in our API documentation.
